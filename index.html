Welcome to Rundown - Fast + Simple

What is Rundown?

A web application that finds related research topics to begin exploring based on frequently cited academic articles.

Is this data reliable?

Yes, we retrieved the data from an api and sorted it to find specific and highly visited articles tailored to help you find the best information in a short amount of time.

What differentiates our site with others?

Rundown searches through to find the most popular academic articles and helps inspire you with your academic endeavours with just a few words.

How do I use it?

  • Type a minimum of 3 words
  • Press enter
  • A list of articles will be shown and you can click each link to learn more
